Annotations (1)

  • comment "Naudet et al. Misalignment can be observed when a system constrains the way another one will be built, structured or will behave. Hence we finally admit that misalignments concern a system on one side and a relation on the other side: between a system and its model (e.g. constraint on the structure); between a model and its representation (e.g. constraint on the syntax of the model); between a system and its objective (e.g. constraint on the behaviour)."
