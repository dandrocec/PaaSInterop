Annotations (1)

  • comment "Naudet et al. Bridging consists in inserting a new system that will serve as an intermediate between inter-related systems causing an interoperability problem. The bridge system is often called an adapter, and it often relies on a translation protocol to actually transfer information from and to interacting systems. It is important to note that bridges might use artefacts that are primarily used for homogenisation. For example, a unified meta-model might be used by a bridge to perform mappings between two models" ()
